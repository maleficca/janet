Simple automation

Janet is a small tool which compiles and runs C# scripts in Revit, just by pressing hotkeys.

Change what you need

Editing scripts, called Blocks is just as simple as editing text files. You can use your favourite text editor to do it!

The power of community

Janet is backed by Revit experts and power-users, eager to help with your automation needs and navigation in the boundless void of Revit.
